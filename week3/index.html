Buenos Aires Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

35 years of history, the purpose of the Buenos Aires Chamber of Commerce project is contributing to the establishment of connections between large and small companies in different areas of interest: business, education, community, entertainment, etc. Furthermore, we have a vast project network to qualify different merchants to expand their business and grow their brand. We are a united community with the same value of making Buenos Aires and our people to grow.
